A Courtesan's Spite

　The story of a Courtesan who holds a grudge against the Clan of Golden Coins.
　To enter this mission, you must meet the following conditions.

I. Four or more Party Members required.
II. All Party Members must be from Black Clans.
III. Achievement level of all Party Members must be at least Cheng 4 of Sun and Moon.
IV. All Party member must have Dan Diyu¨s letter of recommendation.
V. Party Leader should have this tag.
