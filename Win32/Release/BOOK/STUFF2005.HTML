Defeating the forces of The Valley of Hell

　Defeat the forces of The Valley of Hell before they gain too much strength and influence.

　These conditions are required to execute this mission.

 I. Five or more Party members required.
 II. All Party members must be disciples of Heavenly Demon.
 III. Achievement level of all Party Members must be at least Cheng 4 of Raising Light.
 IV. Achievement level of all Party Members cannot exceed Cheng 5 of Five Dragons.
 V. Party Leader should have completed Road to the Dragon [암세출영].
 VI. Party Leader should have this written order.

　Disciples of Heavenly Demon who meet all of these conditions need to go to Ximen Bao, the Follower of Heavenly Demon who resides in Shi Zhang. But if you accept the request, you cannot give up until death.
