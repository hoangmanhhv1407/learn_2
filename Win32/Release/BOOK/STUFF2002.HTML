Defeating the forces of Death Valley

　Defeat the forces of Death Valley before they gain too much strength and influence.

　To attempt this mission, you must meet the following conditions.

 I. Five or more Party Members required.
 II. All Party Members must be disciples of Shaolin.
 III. Achievement level of all Party Members must be at least Cheng 4 of Raising Light.
 IV. Achievement level of all Party Members cannot exceed Cheng 5 of Five Dragons.
 V. Party Leader should have completed [Possession of Cow].
 VI. Party Leader should have this written order.

　Disciples of Shaolin who meet all of these conditions should go to Juejing, the Defender of Shaolin who resides in Zhengzhou. Be warned that if you accept his request, you cannot give up until you complete the mission. . . or die.
