¡¡Good Karma and Bad Karma


Good Karma
Your Good Karma is a reflection of your honor in combat.
You gain your Good Karma automatically based on your battles with Deviant Clans, quest completion and duels with players from hostile clans.


Bad Karma
Unlike Good Karma, an Bad Karma is gained by either malicious activities or cowardly behavior.
Bad Karma is also affected by fighting with, or dying at the hands of, other players.


Titles of Good Karma and Bad Karma
When your Good Karma reaches a certain level you will receive a title.

If you earn the same score for both Good Karma and Bad Karma, then the Bad Karma takes precident
