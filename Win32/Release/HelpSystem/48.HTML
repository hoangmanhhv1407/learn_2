Trading

　 Items can typically be sold to merchants, thrown away or traded with other players.
　Double click on another player to request a trade. Place your items in the trade window and click "Confirm". The trade window will then be locked (red = not confirmed, blue = confirmed). The window will be unlocked if the other party changes an aspect of the trade.
　When both players have clicked the "Confirm" button the trade is complete.
