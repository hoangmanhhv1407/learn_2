Courier¨s Guild and Storage

　 The Courier's Guild can offer you unlimited storage of your items at a reasonable price, and it can also deliver goods for you between their many storehouses throughout The Land.
　In order to store items through the Courier's Guild, you first need to pay a usage fee in gold. To cancel this agreement, you must first remove all of your items from storage, however your usage fee is not refundable.
　You can share storage between different characters in your account.
