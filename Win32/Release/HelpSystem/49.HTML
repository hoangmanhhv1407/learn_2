[Deviant Clan Info]

As Deviant Clan members are your greatest opponents, you may wish to compare your own abilities against theirs before initiating combat. If their name appears in White then they are of a similar level to you. If their name appears in Red then they are of a far higher level than you.


[Character Info]

You can check information on other players stats quite easily.

Click on the player you wish to know about and then click on the "info" button that appears. The other player will be given the choice to "Show All" and may choose to accept or decline your request.

[Brief]
If the other player is not willing to give out information then you can only see their basic stats.
In this case, the information on other players will be displayed in three degrees of information, dependant upon the difference in levels between you and your target.

[Equipment]
If you are both willing it is possible to show the data on your Equipment to each other.
This information is also displayed in three levels, and what you can see depends on the level difference.

[Attributes]
Players can also choose to show their Attributes.
There are 3 varying stages of detail for Attributes as well.

[Show All]
If you both agree then all basic stats, Equipment and Attributes can be viewed.


[Character Menu]

The character menu shows the interactions that can be attempted between players. However, every action (except whisper) can be refused by the other player.
