¡¡ Healing


You can pay any town's Doctor to heal your internal or external wounds, and he can also sell you treatments which will allow you to heal yourself while you are traveling.

Each time you travel you run the risk of being wounded in battle. External wounds will lessen your Life and internal wounds will reduce your Vital Essence. Unless these wounds are treated and healed you will not be able to raise your Life or Vital Energy to their maximum scores.
Consume a self-treatment or visit a Doctor at a local Clinic to have your wounds healed.
