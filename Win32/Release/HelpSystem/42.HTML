Function of Towns

　 Deviant Clans disciples tend to avoid entering towns where Major Clans have settled or Imperial Guards hold posts.
　You can find Merchants in the towns, as well as Doctors, Blacksmiths, Seamstresses, Couriers and Innkeepers. You can chek-in to an Inn to set your Revival Point.
　You will find most quests within Towns.
